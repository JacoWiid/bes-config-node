Welcome To Your Beagle Entertainment System!

This web interface allows you to configure your Beagle Entertainment System (BES) for use. There are three tabs in the upper right section of the page. Click a tab to use that portion of the interface:

  • The left tab allows you to add game ROMs to your BeagleBone Black. It also allows you to remove ROMs that currently exist on your system.
  • The center tab allows you to edit information describing each ROM that you have placed on the system. This ROM information is shown on the BES game selection GUI, and adding it is completely optional.
  • The right tab allows you to configure the gamepads that you use with BES. This involves remapping the buttons and axes on your USB gamepads to match those of the SNES controller.
